The story of our app...

All we can do is our best and sometimes the best that we can do is to start over.

Clientry began with an idea to make it easier for professionals to interact with their clients. From personal trainers to lawyers, our goal was to have all of their needs sorted into one easy to use app!

However, not all things go the way they were planned. Due to multiple team conflicts and a lack of knowledge this project has gone along a bumpy road. We have had multiple renames, multiple redesigns, and we even had to restart from scratch.
Despite all of this, we have worked hard to release what we could in the limited time that we had. After a lot of hard work, Team 33 presents
